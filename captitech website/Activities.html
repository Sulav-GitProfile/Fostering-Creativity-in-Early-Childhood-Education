Learning Activities

Movement & Music Activities

  • 0–2 Years: Tummy Time Dance - Infants track scarves swayed to gentle music, encouraging reaching and kicking (EYLF Outcome 3).
  • 0–2 Years: Shake and Sing - Using baby-safe shakers for call-and-response songs, fostering auditory discrimination.
  • 2–3 Years: Animal Moves Parade - Children imitate animals to music, enhancing coordination and symbolic play.
  • 2–3 Years: Drum Circle - Group drumming to explore tempo and dynamics collaboratively.
  • 3–5 Years: Story Dance - Children interpret picture books through improvised movement sequences.
  • 3–5 Years: Rhythm Building Blocks - Creating sound patterns with percussion instruments, fostering sequencing skills.
  • 6–8 Years: Cultural Dance Workshop - Exploring traditional dances, integrating music, history, and costume.
  • 6–8 Years: Music Video Project - Children choreograph and record dances using tablets, blending performance and digital creativity.

Language & Literacy Activities

  • 0–2 Years: Story Basket Exploration - Infants interact with textured books and puppets during read-alouds (Trevarthen & Aitken, 2001).
  • 0–2 Years: Nursery Rhyme Actions - Singing rhymes with gestures to build sound recognition.
  • 2–3 Years: Puppet Storytelling - Children co-create stories with puppets, enhancing vocabulary and sequencing.
  • 2–3 Years: Sound Hunt - Listening walks to identify and name environmental sounds.
  • 3–5 Years: Story Building Cards - Collaborative storytelling with picture cards to boost narrative skills.
  • 3–5 Years: Role-play Post Office - Writing and delivering “letters” in pretend play to integrate literacy.
  • 6–8 Years: Digital Story Creation - Using Book Creator to write, illustrate, and narrate stories.
  • 6–8 Years: Poetry Café - Children compose and perform original poems, building confidence.

Science Activities

  • 0–2 Years: Water Play - Exploring cause and effect with cups and funnels.
  • 0–2 Years: Sensory Exploration - Investigating leaves, stones, and flowers.
  • 2–3 Years: Mixing Colors - Observing changes with paint and water.
  • 2–3 Years: Planting Seeds - Watering seeds daily to observe growth.
  • 3–5 Years: Sink/Float Investigation - Testing objects to explore buoyancy.
  • 3–5 Years: Insect Observation - Using magnifiers to study insects in the garden.
  • 6–8 Years: Simple Circuits - Building circuits with batteries and bulbs.
  • 6–8 Years: Dissolving Experiments - Testing substances in water to observe changes.

Engineering Activities

  • 0–2 Years: Stacking Soft Blocks - Exploring balance and cause-effect (Cameron et al., 2018).
  • 0–2 Years: Simple Object Sorting - Sorting cups or shapes to build spatial reasoning.
  • 2–3 Years: Cardboard Tube Towers - Building towers to explore stability and height.
  • 2–3 Years: Rolling Ball Ramps - Experimenting with ramps and balls to observe motion.
  • 3–5 Years: Bridge Building Challenge - Designing bridges with LEGO or blocks to hold weight.
  • 3–5 Years: Pulley Play - Lifting objects with basic pulleys to explore mechanics.
  • 6–8 Years: Egg Drop Engineering - Designing protective structures for eggs.
  • 6–8 Years: Simple Circuit Construction - Creating circuits to understand energy flow.

Technologies Activities

  • 0–2 Years: Touch Screen Exploration - Interacting with apps to learn cause and effect.
  • 0–2 Years: Digital Photo Exploration - Using cameras to capture and discuss images.
  • 2–3 Years: Simple Coding with Bee-Bots - Programming directional commands.
  • 2–3 Years: Digital Storytelling - Exploring interactive stories on tablets.
  • 3–5 Years: Creating Digital Art - Using drawing apps to create and share artwork.
  • 3–5 Years: Stop-Motion Animation - Producing simple animations with tablets.
  • 6–8 Years: Multimedia Storytelling - Combining images, audio, and text for digital stories.
  • 6–8 Years: Robotics Challenges - Programming robots to solve tasks.

Mathematics & Numeracy Activities

  • 0–2 Years: Sorting Blocks - Sorting by shape and color.
  • 0–2 Years: Counting Songs - Using finger play, e.g., “Five Little Ducks.”
  • 2–3 Years: Matching Numerals - Matching numeral cards to object sets.
  • 2–3 Years: Tower Heights - Comparing tower heights with simple language.
  • 3–5 Years: Outdoor Shape Hunt - Identifying shapes with clipboards.
  • 3–5 Years: Pattern-Making - Extending sequences with beads.
  • 6–8 Years: Board Game Design - Creating games with addition/subtraction.
  • 6–8 Years: Cooking Fractions - Measuring ingredients to learn fractions.

Humanities & Social Sciences Activities

  • 0–2 Years: Family Photo Exploration - Discussing family relationships with photos.
  • 0–2 Years: Cultural Song Time - Singing songs from different cultures.
  • 2–3 Years: Community Helpers Role-Play - Acting out roles like firefighters or doctors.
  • 2–3 Years: Story Sack Exploration - Using props to discuss social interactions.
  • 3–5 Years: Map Your Playground - Creating simple maps of classrooms or playgrounds.
  • 3–5 Years: Cultural Festival Day - Exploring customs, dress, and food.
  • 6–8 Years: Historical Diary Project - Writing diaries as historical figures.
  • 6–8 Years: Social Problem-Solving - Designing solutions for community issues like recycling.

Integrated Curriculum Activities

  • 0–2 Years: Sensory Engineering Boxes - Exploring water, sand, and soft materials.
  • 0–2 Years: Sound Exploration - Creating sounds with instruments and objects.
  • 2–3 Years: Building Simple Structures - Stacking blocks to explore balance.
  • 2–3 Years: Colour Mixing Lab - Mixing paints to discover color changes.
  • 3–5 Years: Design a Playground - Building mini playgrounds with blocks.
  • 3–5 Years: Simple Coding Games - Using Bee-Bot for sequencing challenges.
  • 6–8 Years: Bridge Engineering & Art - Designing and decorating bridges.
  • 6–8 Years: Environmental STEAM Project - Addressing issues like waste management.
